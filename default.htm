Who is Wakow?

Haphazardly derived from the phrase : "Who Knows What", Wakow is a bot created to help you and your team connect and identify internal experts using #Slack!

The Technology

Wakow is powered by Microsoft's LUIS and Bot Framework. Written in C# and taking full advantage of the Azure cloud platform, Wakow services are designed to grow seemlessly as your buisiness does.

The Team

Wakow was created by a happy team of three engineers : Benjamin Brodeur Mathieu, Francis Richard-Emond and Tommy Sagala.

Get Answers!

Just type : "I need help with general topic" and let Wakow find the right person to help you. He will then ask you for a more "specific question" so you can get help tailored to your needs. The only thing you have to do is grade the incoming answers to make Wakow learn. It's that easy.

Find Expertise

Know who knows what by asking questions in the form of "Does @username know about topic" or "What does @username know about ?".

Genuine interactions

Salute and thank Wakow to enable richer and more believable interactions. Even thought he can be rough at times Wakow truly loves you.
