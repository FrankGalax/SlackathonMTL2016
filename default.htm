Wakow

Who is Wakow?

Haphazardly derived from the phrase : "Who Knows What", Wakow is a bot created to help you and your team connect and identify internal experts using #Slack!

The Technology

Wakow is powered by Microsoft's LUIS and Bot Framework. Written in C# and taking full advantage of the Azure cloud platform, Wakow services are designed to grow seemlessly as your buisiness does.

The Team

Wakow was created by a happy team of three engineers : Benjamin Brodeur Mathieu, Francis Richard-Emond and Tommy Sagala.
